The GoFlow integration lets you connect your existing GoFlow account to nocnoc, enabling automated order management between both platforms. Once connected, nocnoc orders flow directly into GoFlow so you can manage fulfillment from a single place.

## How it works

GoFlow acts as the middleware between your sales channels and nocnoc. Once your nocnoc store is connected in GoFlow, you can import your listings and start receiving nocnoc orders directly in GoFlow for fulfillment.

The setup involves two main steps: connecting your nocnoc store to GoFlow, and then enabling and creating your nocnoc listings. Your account manager will guide you through the connection — after that, you manage everything from GoFlow.
