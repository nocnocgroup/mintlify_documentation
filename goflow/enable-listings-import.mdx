---
title: "Enable listings import"
description: "How to enable listings import for your nocnoc store in GoFlow."
---

Before you can create or manage nocnoc listings in GoFlow, you need to enable listings import for your nocnoc store. This allows GoFlow to automatically pull listing data from nocnoc at regular intervals.

## To enable listings import

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open your nocnoc store">
    From the left sidebar, go to **Directory → Stores** and open your nocnoc store.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Edit Listings Import">
    On the **Status** card, find the **Listings Import** setting and click the pencil icon to edit.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enable and save">
    Set Listings Import to **Enabled** and click **Save**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Once enabled, GoFlow will sync listings from nocnoc every few hours. Any updates made to a listing on the nocnoc side will be reflected in GoFlow during the next import.
