## Integration flow

<Steps>
  <Step title="Authentication" icon="key">
    Get your `SELLER_KEY` and set the `X-Api-Key` header on every request. [Learn how →](/authentication)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Publish products" icon="package">
    Create and sync your catalog with up-to-date stock and pricing. You can create products manually or from an Amazon ASIN. [Learn how →](/api-reference/products/create-product)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Manage orders" icon="shopping-cart">
    Retrieve and process incoming orders from all your channels. Filter by status, date, or order ID. [Learn how →](/api-reference/orders/list-orders)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Ship packages" icon="truck">
    Register Son packages per order and consolidate them into a Father package for bulk first-mile shipping. [Learn how →](/guides/sending-packages)
  </Step>
</Steps>
